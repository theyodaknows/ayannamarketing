How It Works

01

Discovery & Brief

We start with a call to understand your event, your brand, your goals, and what "success" looks like in content terms. What platforms? What formats? What story are we telling?

02

Pre-Production Planning

Shot list, run of show, talent access coordination, equipment planning, and a clear content brief so we arrive knowing exactly what we need to capture — and why.

03

Day-of Production

We show up early, move seamlessly through your event or set, and capture everything on brief. For live social packages, content goes live during the event itself.

04

Post-Production & Delivery

Editing, color grading, format optimization, and delivery. Turnaround depends on package — same-day cuts for social, full-length edits within 7–14 business days.

05

Distribution Strategy

We don't just hand you a file. We give you a deployment plan — when to post, what format per platform, how to maximize shelf life from one production day.

Content you can actually use — not just archive.

The biggest waste in production isn't a bad shot. It's beautiful footage that never gets posted because no one planned the distribution. We plan both on the same day.
