Scope of Work

What your Fractional CMO actually does.

This isn't consulting. It's embedded leadership. Here's what Ayanna owns when she's in your corner.

01

Marketing Strategy & Roadmap

Quarterly marketing plans tied to your business goals. No more guessing what to do next — you'll have a clear, prioritized roadmap with owners and timelines.

02

Brand Infrastructure

Positioning, messaging hierarchy, visual standards, voice guidelines. The foundational system that makes every piece of content work harder.

03

Team Leadership & Vendor Management

Ayanna leads your internal marketing team and manages external agencies, freelancers, and production vendors — so you're not the bottleneck.

04

Campaign Oversight & Execution

From concept to launch to post-mortem. Campaigns built with clear KPIs, managed through production, and measured with rigor.

05

Revenue & Growth Analytics

We connect every marketing activity to a revenue outcome. Monthly reporting that the CEO, board, and investors actually care about.

06

AI Integration & Tech Stack

We identify and implement the right tools — AI content production, CRM, analytics, automation — so your marketing operates at speed and scale.
