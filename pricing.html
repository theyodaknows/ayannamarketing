Common questions.

Is there a minimum contract length?

Most social media management packages start with a 3-month commitment, which gives us the time to build momentum and show measurable results. Month-to-month options are available after that period.

What's not included in the monthly fee?

Ad spend is always billed separately — you pay the platforms directly. Some packages include ad management (the strategy and execution) but not the media budget itself. Any production costs above the package scope are discussed upfront.

How do I know which package is right for me?

Book a free discovery call. We'll ask about your goals, current situation, and budget — and we'll recommend the right starting point honestly, even if it's not the most expensive option.

Do you work with clients outside Los Angeles?

Yes. The majority of our social media management and strategy clients are remote. We work with businesses and brands across the country. Event production and on-site work are primarily Los Angeles-based.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my package?

Yes — with 30 days' notice. We want the engagement to be the right size for your business, and we'll proactively suggest adjustments if we think you'd benefit from scaling up or down.

What makes you different from other agencies?

We've operated at the celebrity and major network level — that's a different standard of quality, discretion, and strategic thinking. We bring that same bar to every client regardless of budget. And unlike most agencies, you get a real strategist, not just a content scheduler.
